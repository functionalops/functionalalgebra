Fun(ctional) Algebra

Goals

The goals of this website and accompanying material (e.g. code, slides, etc.) are:

  • Broad Audience - make this kind of material accessible to developers who have a primarily imperative, non-functional background. Many developers who have spent time writing object oriented code likely have this background.
  • Practical - offer small but non-trivial code examples to support claims about thinking about code functionally and using algebraic properties to improve various aspects of your codebase.
  • Digestible - the reader will not be required to have a background in algebra for the beginner articles and any prerequisite knowledge of algebra in non-beginner articles will be linked to or provided within the article. I only have an undergraduate degree in Mathematics so my discussion of abstract algebra in these articles will not be particularly formal, for the reason of making the content as digestible as possible.
  • Informal - the style of writing here will be fairly informal. I may use slang words or pop culture references ocassionally. I may even include meme images as a form of light comic relief. My hope is that this content will not be too boring or dry to digest.
